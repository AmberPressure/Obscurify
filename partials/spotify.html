How your data is used

To use Obscurify, on the next page you'll need to allow the app to have access to some of your account information

The only data it records is your username (so it can keep track of your score) and the country your account is registered to

Obscurify won't ever email you, and does not record your email address or profile image

All data is solely used to display the most accurate representation of the Spotify listening community
